# Join Us

We are always looking for motivated researchers interested in climate-ecosystem interactions, remote sensing, and environmental data science.

## Current Openings

- **PhD Students** — Applications for Fall 2025 are welcome
- **Postdoctoral Researchers** — Positions available for candidates with expertise in remote sensing, land surface modeling, or atmospheric science
- **Undergraduate Research** — Opportunities for NYU undergraduates interested in research experience

## What We Look For

- Interest in climate change impacts on ecosystems
- Quantitative skills (programming, statistics, modeling)
- Experience with remote sensing, GIS, or Earth system data is a plus
- Curiosity and collaborative spirit

## How to Apply

**Prospective PhD students:** Apply through [NYU Environmental Studies](https://as.nyu.edu/departments/environment.html). Mention Prof. Yi Yin in your application.

**Postdocs:** Email Yi directly with your CV, a brief research statement, and 2-3 representative publications.

**Undergraduates:** Email Yi to discuss potential projects.
