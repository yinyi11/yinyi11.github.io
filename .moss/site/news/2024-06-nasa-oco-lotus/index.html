New NASA OCO-LOTUS project funded

2024 · 6 · 1

Yi has been awarded a NASA Orbiting Carbon Observatory 2/3 Science Team project as Institutional-PI. The project, "Optimizing Carbon Observations: Long-Term Understanding and Synthesis (OCO-LOTUS)," is funded at $670k for 2024–2027 and aims to advance our understanding of the global carbon cycle through satellite observations.
