Our paper [Recent methane surges reveal heightened emissions from tropical inundated areas](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-00000-0) is now published in *Nature Communications*.

This study identifies tropical inundated areas as a key source of recent methane surges, highlighting the sensitivity of wetland methane emissions to climate variability.
