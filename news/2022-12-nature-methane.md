New paper in *Nature*: [Wetland emission and atmospheric sink changes explain methane growth in 2020](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-022-05447-w).

This collaborative study identifies key drivers of the accelerating methane growth rate, showing that wetland emissions and atmospheric sink changes played major roles in the 2020 methane surge.
