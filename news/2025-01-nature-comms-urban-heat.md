Our paper [Contemporary income inequality outweighs historic redlining in shaping intra-urban heat disparities in Los Angeles](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-025-00000-0) is now published in *Nature Communications*.

Led by postdoc Anamika Shreevastava, this study reveals that present-day income inequality is a stronger predictor of intra-urban heat disparities in Los Angeles than historical redlining. The findings underscore the need for climate adaptation policies that address current socioeconomic inequities, not just historical ones.
