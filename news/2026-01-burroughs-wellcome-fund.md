Yi has been awarded a Burroughs Wellcome Fund Climate Change and Human Health Seed Grant for the project "Moving Into Harm's Way? Paradox of Domestic Migration, Climate Extremes, and Health System Readiness." This project investigates the intersection of domestic migration patterns, exposure to climate extremes, and health system capacity.
