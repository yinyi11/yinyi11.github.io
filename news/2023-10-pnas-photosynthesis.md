Our paper [The weekly cycle of photosynthesis in Europe reveals the negative impact of particulate pollution on carbon uptake](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2306507120) is now published in *Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences*.

By analyzing the weekly cycle of solar-induced fluorescence across Europe, this study demonstrates that particulate air pollution significantly reduces vegetation photosynthesis — providing new evidence linking air quality to carbon cycle impacts.
